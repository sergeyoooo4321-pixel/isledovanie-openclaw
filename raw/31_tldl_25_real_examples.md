## Common Failure Patterns
1. **Over-automating early:** Start human-in-loop before removing checkpoints
2. **Ignoring context windows:** Narrow scopes outperform broad queries
3. **Skipping evaluation:** Top users sample 10-20% of outputs weekly to catch degradation
