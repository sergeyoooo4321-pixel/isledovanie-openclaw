## Common Pitfalls & Lessons Learned
- **Runaway costs:** One user's unbounded coding agent consumed $1000+ tokens
- **Accuracy issues:** AI summarization can miss critical details or hallucinate
- **Platform limits:** Social media APIs have rate limits; bank integrations require extreme security
- **Trust calibration:** Best results maintaining 60-80% automation with human oversight
